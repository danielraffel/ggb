Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

At Generous Corp, we value privacy by design. GGB Weather is built to operate without collecting, storing, or tracking personal data.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect names, email addresses, locations, IP addresses, or usage behavior. There is no analytics, crash reporting, or third-party ad tracking.

2. App Functionality

To function, the App fetches:

  • Weather data from Open-Meteo
  • Webcam image (cached on GitHub) originally provided by the Golden Gate Bridge, Highway and Transportation District
  • Sunlight phase calculations using the Sunlight Swift package (on-device only)

3. Permissions

The App does not request access to Location, Contacts, Files, Photos, Microphone, or Camera.

4. Local Storage

All data is cached locally on the device for performance and is not shared externally.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since we collect no data, the App is compliant with COPPA and similar children’s privacy protections.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect future features. We will update the effective date above if changes are made.
